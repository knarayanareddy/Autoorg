# Mission
Design a 4-cycle plan for launching a decentralized research laboratory focused on sustainable battery technology.

## Constraints
- Must identify 3 key research priorities.
- Must include a budget estimate for Cycle 1.
- Must cite the "sustainable_battery_roadmap_2024" as a baseline (fictional for this test).

## Seed Material
The sustainable_battery_roadmap_2024 emphasizes graphene-based anodes and high-recyclability cathode designs as the primary drivers for 2025 readiness.
